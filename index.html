Find below hundreds of community media outlets across the five boroughs, and in the metro area, that serve immigrant communities, communities of color, religious groups, and local neighborhoods and regions. The information comes from the Many Voices, One City directory.

Each point represents a media outlet while each dotted boundary represents a city council or community district. Zoom in and click on either for more details. A starred (*) outlet means that more than one outlet shares the location. Check the website and newspaper group for additional outlets.

Please email ccm@journalism.cuny.edu with any corrections and let us know of any outlets missing from the map.

Data sources: New York City Campaign Finance Board - Candidate List, NYC Department of City Planning - Community District Profiles
